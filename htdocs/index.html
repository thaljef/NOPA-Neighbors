Howdy Neighbor!

My wife and I have lived at 38 Cole Street since 2007. We love looking at the beautiful buildings and meeting all the wonderful people who live in this part of the NOrth PAnhandle neighborhood. But sometimes, the wind and rain and a few careless citizens make a mess. It saddens us when we see the neighborhood littered with trash and debris. But with your help, we’d like do something about it.

The NOPA Neighbors Fund

Using the button below, you can contribute to the NOPA Neighbors Fund. Each time the Fund reaches $300, I will use the money to hire day-laborers to help me clean the neighborhood. We’ll spend an entire day removing trash, sweeping sidewalks, picking up dog poop, pulling weeds, and whatever else needs to be done to keep our streets safe and clean.

Think Globally, Act Locally

As residents and homeowners, I bet most of us wouldn’t mind paying a little extra in city taxes or fees if we knew the money would be spent for services in our neighborhood.

100% of the NOPA Neighbors Fund will go towards cleaning the area bounded by Schrader, Clayton, Fulton, and Fell Streets. So your contribution will have a direct impact in your immediate area.

At the same time, you’ll be helping to make the entire neighborhood safer and more desirable.

Love Thy Neighbor

This is not an official charity, organization, or institution. I’m just a friendly neighbor trying to make our corner of the world a little better. I hope we’ll repeatedly load the Fund so we can clean the neighborhood several times a year. But if the Fund does not reach the $300 goal within 3 months, I’ll refund all money to the donors.

To make a donation using a credit card, enter an amount and click the "Donate" button below. Your payment is securely handled by Google -- I don't receive any of your financial information. However, I do use your e-mail address to notify you when the clean-up will happen. If you'd like to donate by cash, check, or other means, or if you have questions about the NOPA Neighbors Fund, you are welcome to contact me.

I thank you in advance for your time, and your donation!   -Jeff
